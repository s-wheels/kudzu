Mission

Kudzu (/ˈkʊdzuː/)(/ˈkʊdzuː/) is a group of plants in the genus Pueraria, in the pea family Fabaceae, subfamily Faboideae. They are climbing, coiling, and trailing perennial vines native to much of eastern Asia, Southeast Asia, and some Pacific islands.

Fast Growth

Kudzu grows at an incredible rate. Rapidly growing in new areas.

Versatile

Persistent

Kudzu is notoriously hard to kill, it is persistent and thrives through difficulty.
